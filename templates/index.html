Key Features


Encryption and Decryption Options
Users can select between encryption or decryption tasks, enabling them to safeguard sensitive information or retrieve encrypted data.


Input Flexibility
The platform allows users to input text directly or upload text files for encryption or decryption purposes, ensuring convenience and versatility.


Algorithm Selection
Users can choose from a range of encryption algorithms, each tailored to specific security requirements and preferences. Options may include AES, RSA, DES, and more.


Customizable Parameters
Depending on the chosen encryption algorithm, users are prompted to enter necessary parameters such as keys, initialization vectors, or other algorithm-specific configurations.


Real-time Attack Detection
Our project incorporates advanced security measures to detect and respond to various cyber attacks. Intrusion detection systems continuously monitor system activities.


Attack Simulation
To demonstrate the effectiveness of our security measures, the platform includes a feature for simulating common cyber attacks. Users can initiate attacks such as SQL injection.


Notification System
Upon detecting a potential cyber attack, the platform sends immediate notifications to designated administrators or users, ensuring prompt action and minimizing potential damage.


Attack Logging and Analysis
Detailed logs of detected attacks are maintained for analysis purposes, allowing administrators to identify patterns, assess vulnerabilities, and enhance overall security posture.


Educational Resources
To promote cybersecurity awareness and knowledge, the platform provides educational resources, including tutorials, articles, and best practices for preventing and mitigating cyber threats.
